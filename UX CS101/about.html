Who are we? Laura and Tim

In May 2018 Laura and Tim took over Snow Denn Lodge. Plenty of inspiration and a new, fresh vibe has been brought to Snow Denn Lodge, which is slowly being updated and rejuvenated. With a passion for all things outdoors and participating in nearly every activity that Methven & Mt Hutt have to offer these two are the people you want to talk to about things to see and do in the area.

We have... Happy Guests

During the Winter we have had some amazing guests stay with us and enjoy the beautiful Winter we have had up Mt Hutt. We look forward to sharing the Summer with many more guests who wish to make the most of Methven's stunning walks, hikes and Mountain Bike trails.

Our favourites... Methven's Summer Attractions

If you haven't experie nced Methven and Mt Hutt in Summer you are missing out. With our outstanding DownHill and Cross-Country Mountain bike tracks which have been developed up Mt Hutt there's not a better place to be and why not relax and unwind at the brand new Opuke Hot Pools located just 5 minutes from the lodge. Along with this we have some of the most beautiful clear blue lakes which host an abundance of fish. And some of New Zealand's best walks can be found here along with the Te Arora trail sidling along the side of Mt Hutt.

So.. Why choose us?

You might ask what set us apart from all the other accommodation places in Methven. Well let me tell you, with young, inspired owners these Lodges have a great, fun, relaxed vibe. We like to interact with our guests to offer our knowledge of the area so you can enjoy it as much as possible. We also strive everyday to keep our accommodation up to a high standard that provides you with maximum comfort and relaxation during your stay, and best of all we have the largest variety of rooms so there's a room to suit everyone's choice and budget.
