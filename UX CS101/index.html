Private Lodge & Holiday Home

Thanks for visiting!

Situated in front of the Southern Alps, Snow Denn Lodge is Methven's premier lodge, boasting superior facilities with a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and requirements. With the Great Outdoors and amazing scenic views right on your doorstep, it makes for a popular holiday destination all year round.

Here at Snow Denn, we have something for everybody.

One lodge is run as a Holiday Home perfect for Families and Friends wanting space to relax and enjoy each other's company. The other lodge is a Private Family Friendly lodge containing Superking with en-suite rooms, a family room and a twin bunk room along with some permanent accommodation rooms.

Two Separate Lodges

Within a stone's throw away to local the new Opuke Hot Pools, Cafes, Supermarkets, Bars, Restaurants, Mt Hutt Ski Field, Mt Hutt Bike Park, Golf courses and recreational facilities.

In the centre of Methven

Enjoy unlimited WiFi, for those that want to stay in contact with their folks at home, and those that can't miss their favourite TV series.
